About CAKE"O"CLOCK (Our Flagship Store)

The establishment is noted for its **large, spacious, and pleasant atmosphere** with **ample seating**, making it suitable for groups of friends or families to relax and socialize. It features a massive display counter showcasing its extensive range of products. The location is strategic, situated on the Mettupalayam Main Road in the Donington area, directly **opposite the Vinayagar Temple**, and is hard to miss.

Offerings and Specialities

  • **Cakes and Pastries:** A huge variety of options, including popular choices like **honey cake, chocolate, black forest, and red velvet cakes**. Customers can also order **customized cakes** for special occasions.
  • **Snacks and Savouries:** The shelves are stacked with a wide selection of goodies, such as various biscuits, puffs, cupcakes, sandwiches, burgers, chicken items, soups, noodles, and pizza.
  • **Beverages:** They offer a wide range of hot and cold drinks, with their **ginger tea (without milk)** and coffee being particularly popular and reasonably priced.
  • **Nilgiri Delicacies:** A must-try item is the local specialty, **ghee varkey** (a type of biscuit/cookie), which many visitors purchase to take home.

Services and Reputation

Friends Cake O' Clock provides a variety of services to its customers, including:

  • **Dine-in** and **Take-out** options.
  • **Home delivery** and **no-contact delivery** services.
  • The option to order **customized cakes**.

The establishment enjoys a generally **positive reputation** for its quick service, good quantity of food, friendly staff, and tasty items. Overall, it is highly recommended as a great place to satisfy sweet cravings and enjoy a diverse selection of food items.
